Benefits of ML Systems

ML systems are able to learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed to do so. They are able to generalize from the training data to new, unseen data. They are able to handle large amounts of data and complex relationships between variables. They are able to learn from data in a way that is not possible for humans.

  • Humans can learn from the patterns gathered by the model
  • They can tacke problems that are not possible to solve with traditional algorithms
